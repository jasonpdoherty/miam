# ⚽ TRAINING - 08/12/25

**60 minutes | Sprint Competition + Ball Work**

---

## 1. WARM-UP (3 min)
2 laps + dynamic stretches

---

## 2. SHUTTLE SPRINT RACES (12 min)

**Pairs race - Cones at 10m, 20m, 30m**

**6 rounds:** 10m/20m/30m/10m/20m/30m (there and back each)
- Touch each cone with hand
- Winner = 1 point
- 40s rest between

---

## 3. RONDO (15 min)

**10x10 grids, groups of 5-6**

**4 rounds × 4 min:**
1. 4v2, two-touch
2. 5v2, one-touch
3. 4v2, count passes - 10 in a row = defenders do 5 press-ups
4. 5v2, two-touch, switch defenders every 45s

---

## 4. SHOOTING IN PAIRS (18 min)

**Both nets, same pairs**

1. **Alternate shooting** (5 min) - 2 touches, set and shoot
2. **Lay-off and shoot** (5 min) - one lays off, partner shoots
3. **Turn and shoot** (4 min) - back to goal, turn, shoot. Best of 5, loser = 5 press-ups
4. **First time finish** (4 min) - serve from angle, first time finish
