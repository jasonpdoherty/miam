# ⚽ TRAINING - 03/11/25

**60 minutes | Simple & Competitive**

---

## 1. WARM-UP (3 min)
2 laps + stretches

---

## 2. FITNESS CIRCUIT (18 min)

**Setup:** Cones at 10m, 20m, 30m, 40m

**Split into 2 pairs (A vs B)**
One pair runs, other does strength, then switch

**8 ROUNDS:**

**R1:** Pair A runs to 10m cone | Pair B does squats 45s
**R2:** Pair B runs to 10m cone | Pair A does squats 45s

**R3:** Pair A runs to 20m cone | Pair B does press-ups 45s
**R4:** Pair B runs to 20m cone | Pair A does press-ups 45s

**R5:** Pair A runs to 30m cone | Pair B plank hold
**R6:** Pair B runs to 30m cone | Pair A plank hold

**R7:** Pair A runs to 40m cone | Pair B does burpees 45s
**R8:** Pair B runs to 40m cone | Pair A does burpees 45s

**SCORING:**
- Fastest on each run = 1 point
- Highest combined total on strength = 1 point
- Most points wins overall

30s rest between rounds

---

## 3. PASSING COMP (10 min)

**New pairs, 15m apart**

Most successful passes in 60s

**Round 1:** Both feet
**Round 2:** Weak foot only
**Round 3:** Both feet, faster

Winners stay together, losers swap partners

30s rest between rounds

---

## 4. SHOOTING COMP (12 min)

**Edge of box, take turns**

12 shots total per player (4 shots × 3 rounds)

**Round 1:** Placed finish (accuracy)
**Round 2:** Power finish (laces)
**Round 3:** Weak foot only

Keep individual scores
Announce leader after each round

---

## 5. MATCH (17 min)

Balanced teams
Bragging rights on the line
