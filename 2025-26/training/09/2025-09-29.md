# ⚽ TRAINING SESSION - 2025-09-29

**60 minutes | Fitness + Agility + Core Training**

---

## 1. WARM-UP (5 min)
- Light jog 3 laps + dynamic stretches

---

## 2. KNEE TAG WARS (9 min)

**Setup:** 15m × 15m area per group

**Rounds 1-3 (4.5 min): PAIRS**
- 1 minute work / 30 seconds rest
- Partners face each other
- Score points by touching opponent's knee
- Keep individual scores

**Rounds 4-6 (4.5 min): GROUPS OF 3**
- 1 minute work / 30 seconds rest
- Watch 2 opponents at once - protect your knees while attacking theirs
- Keep individual scores within your group of 3
- Winner of each group = bragging rights!

---

## 3. FITNESS - DRIBBLE DELIVERY & COLLECTION (18 min)

**Setup:** START ——15m—— LINE 1 ——10m—— LINE 2 (25m total)
**Return path:** 4-5 cones, 2-3m apart for weaving

**8 rounds, 10 seconds rest between rounds**
**Partners alternate who goes first each round**

**Sequence each round:**

**Player 1 (Delivery Phase):**
1. Dribble ball 1 to 15m line - stop it DEAD on the line
2. Sprint back (no ball)
3. Dribble ball 2 to 25m line - stop it DEAD on the line
4. Sprint back (no ball)

**Player 2 (Collection Phase):**
1. Sprint to 15m line, collect ball 1
2. Dribble back weaving through cones
3. Sprint to 25m line, collect ball 2
4. Dribble back weaving through cones

**Penalties:**
- Ball doesn't stop dead on line = Player 1 must go reset it properly
- Miss a cone on weave = Player 2 must go back and do it again

**Rounds 1, 3, 5, 7:** Player A delivers, Player B collects
**Rounds 2, 4, 6, 8:** Player B delivers, Player A collects

---

## 4. FINISHING - REACTIVE SHOOTING (12 min)

**Setup:** Penalty area, 3-4 servers positioned at different angles/distances (mix of wide and central, varying depths)

**Format:**
- 3-4 shooters in the box at a time
- Servers pass randomly - shooters don't know who's passing next
- Must be alert and react to incoming ball
- 1-2 touch finish maximum
- Rotate shooters every 2-3 minutes

**Emphasis:** 
- Stay switched on, scan for the pass
- Quick reactions
- Composure under surprise
- See the ball hit the net - build confidence

---

## 5. MATCH (19 min)

**Setup:** Full area, balanced teams

**Emphasis:** 
- Use your agility to beat opponents
- Quick changes of direction  
- Win every 50-50 with fitness advantage
- Proper battles, aggressive but fair
