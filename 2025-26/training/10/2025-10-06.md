# ⚽ TRAINING SESSION - 2025-10-06

**60 minutes | Fitness + Shooting + Box Awareness**

---

## 1. WARM-UP (5 min)
- Light jog 2 laps + dynamic stretches

---

## 2. FITNESS - SHOOTING SHUTTLES (15 min)

**Setup:** START ——15m—— CONE A ——10m—— CONE B (Edge of box)
Ball station at Cone B, goal with keeper

**8 rounds, 20 seconds rest between rounds**

**Each round:**
1. Sprint to Cone A, touch it
2. Sprint back to START, touch line
3. Sprint to Cone B, collect ball
4. SHOOT (1-2 touches max)
5. Sprint back to START

**Variations (2 rounds each):**
- Right foot finish only
- Left foot finish only
- First touch across body, then shoot
- Direct strike, no touch

**Emphasis:** Max effort sprints, composed finish even when tired

---

## 3. BOX AWARENESS - CHAOS CROSSING (15 min)

**Setup:** Penalty area, 2-3 servers wide on each side with balls

**Format:** 4-6 players in the box constantly
- Servers deliver crosses randomly from either side
- Players don't know which server is crossing next
- MUST stay alert, scan constantly
- Attack every cross aggressively
- Rotate servers every 3-4 minutes

**Service variations:**
- Low driven crosses
- High floated crosses
- Cut-backs from byline
- Early crosses from deep

**Focus:**
- Read the flight early
- Attack the ball, don't wait for it
- Check shoulder to see where ball is coming from
- Win your space in the box

---

## 4. FITNESS - BALL RECOVERY SPRINTS (5 min)

**Setup:** 25m channel, balls spread at random distances

**Format:** Pairs competition
- Balls placed at 10m, 15m, 20m, 25m
- On whistle, sprint to collect assigned ball and dribble back
- First pair back wins
- 5 rounds, 30s rest between

**Makes it work with fewer players - everyone works hard!**

---

## 5. MATCH (20 min)

**Setup:** Full area, balanced teams (even if small-sided)

**Emphasis:** 
- Use your fitness advantage from earlier work
- Be alert in the box like in training
- Quick transitions
- Proper battles for every ball
