# ⚽ TRAINING - 10/11/25

**60 minutes | Fitness Focus + Team Bonding**

---

## 1. WARM-UP (3 min)
2 laps + dynamic stretches

---

## 2. PYRAMID SPRINTS (12 min)

**Setup:** Cones at 20m, 40m, 60m

**Working in pairs - races against other pair**

**SEQUENCE:**
- Sprint to 20m and back
- Sprint to 40m and back  
- Sprint to 60m and back
- Sprint to 40m and back
- Sprint to 20m and back

**SCORING:**
- First pair back each round = 1 point
- Most points after 3 full pyramids wins

**30s rest between pyramids**

---

## 3. PARTNER FITNESS (15 min)

**Stay in same pairs**

**6 STATIONS × 90s each:**

**Station 1:** BALL THROWS
- Partner A throws ball to Partner B's chest
- Partner B catches and throws back
- Count successful catches

**Station 2:** PIGGYBACK RELAY
- One carries, one rides
- To cone 20m away and back
- Swap halfway

**Station 3:** BALL SLAMS
- Partner A holds ball overhead, slams to ground
- Partner B catches on bounce, slams back
- Full power, count total slams

**Station 4:** BACK-TO-BACK SQUATS
- Partners stand back-to-back, link arms
- Both squat down together and back up
- Count total successful squats

**Station 5:** PLANK HIGH-FIVES
- Both in plank position facing each other
- High-five opposite hands (left to right)
- Count total high-fives

**Station 6:** BURPEE PASS
- Both do burpee
- At top, chest pass ball to partner
- Count successful passes

**30s rest between stations**

**SCORING:** Most combined reps/fastest times = pair with most points wins

---

## 4. PASS AND MOVE TRIANGLES (15 min)

**Groups of 3, one ball per group**

**Setup:** Triangle shape, 10m between players

**DRILL:**
- Player A passes to B, follows pass
- Player B receives, passes to C, follows pass  
- Player C receives, passes to A, follows pass
- Keep pattern going - stay sharp

**PROGRESSION 1 (3 min):** Walk through pattern
**PROGRESSION 2 (3 min):** Jog the pattern, two-touch
**PROGRESSION 3 (3 min):** Match pace, one-touch if possible
**PROGRESSION 4 (3 min):** Add second ball - both directions
**PROGRESSION 5 (3 min):** Race - which group gets most passes in 60s

**Minimal rest**

---

## 5. MATCH (15 min)

**Make teams from the pairs/groups**
Play with intensity
Bragging rights
